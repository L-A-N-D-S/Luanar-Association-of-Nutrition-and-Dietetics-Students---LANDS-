About Us

The Luanar Association of Nutrition and Dietetics Students (LANDS) is a non-profitable student organisation composed of students from the Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources (LUANAR), bringing together members from both the Natural Resources Campus and Bunda Campus.

Our goal is to promote awareness and knowledge in nutrition and dietetics, support student development, and contribute positively to community health through education and engagement.

LANDS is open to anyone who is interested in nutrition and dietetics. We encourage students to join, participate, and grow together as future professionals in the field.

We are also looking for partnerships with other organisations, institutions, and stakeholders who are willing to support and collaborate with a student-based initiative like ours.
